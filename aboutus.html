About Us

Our Purpose

Elida Apartments is owned by a hardworking mother from Davao City. It aims to provide affordable housing with a good environment and spacious parking in the North of Davao City where most students, families, and employees look for an apartment that have access to everything and close to the workplace or schools.

Room Information

Studio Normal New building

There are 10 normal studio units in the new building. Each room has its own kitchen, bathroom, and free parking privilege for one vehicle. Rent is Php 6000 or about 100 usd, exclusive of utilities, paid monthly. Two months advance, one month deposit are required.

Studio Loft Type New Building First Floor

There are 5 loft-type studio units in the new building. Each room has its own loft-type bed space, kitchen, bathroom, and free parking privilege for one vehicle. Rent is Php 10,000 or about 200 usd, exclusive of utilities, paid monthly. Two months advance, one month deposit are required.

One bedroom Old building

There are 5 one-bedroom units from first building. These bedrooms are good as new and are more spacious and cheaper. It has its own room, bathroom, and kitchen area.
